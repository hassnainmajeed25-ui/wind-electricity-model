Wind Energy System

Wind energy is a renewable source of power that converts moving air into electricity. It is clean, sustainable, and widely used in modern power generation systems.

How It Works

Wind turns the turbine blades, which rotate a shaft connected to a generator. The generator converts mechanical energy into electrical energy.

This electricity is then supplied to homes, industries, and power grids. Wind energy reduces pollution and dependence on fossil fuels.
