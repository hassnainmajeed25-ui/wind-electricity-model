About This Project

The Wind Electricity Model is an educational project designed to explain how wind energy is converted into electrical power. Wind energy is one of the most important renewable energy sources in the world because it is clean, sustainable, and environmentally friendly.

In this project, we demonstrate how wind turbines capture the kinetic energy of moving air and convert it into mechanical energy. This mechanical energy is then transformed into electrical energy using a generator system.

Our goal is to provide a clear understanding of wind power systems, circuit connections, and simulation models. This helps students and beginners learn how renewable energy systems work in real life.

This project also includes visual simulation ideas and circuit diagrams to make learning more interactive and practical. It is designed as a group effort to improve teamwork and technical understanding.
