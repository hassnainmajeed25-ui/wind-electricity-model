Wind Electricity Model

Wind energy is one of the cleanest renewable energy sources.
It converts wind movement into mechanical energy using turbines.
This mechanical energy is then converted into electricity.
Wind power helps reduce pollution and saves natural resources.
In this project we explain turbine systems, circuits and simulation models.
Our aim is to understand how wind becomes usable electricity.
